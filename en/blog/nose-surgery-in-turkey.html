Facts about rhinoplasty in Turkey

Rhinoplasty is a type of plastic & cosmetic surgery that involves changing the shape of the nose. Many aspects can be changed, such as the structure of the nose and its aesthetic appearance. You can also change the size; we mean here making the nose bigger or smaller or change the direction of the tip of the nose or its angle.

Rhinoplasty is not only for aesthetic purposes. Some people have a deviated septum, which causes shortness of breath(dyspnea). Rhinoplasty can adjust the nose, nasal structure and improve the patient's life.

The advantages of rhinoplasty in Turkey

Rhinoplasty not only enhances the appearance and function of the nose but also boosts self-confidence.

Additionally, rhinoplasty surgery works on:

  • Increasing or reducing the size of the nose.
  • A new shape of the nostrils.
  • Reducing the nasal tip.
  • Reducing the nasal bridge, which is the part above the nasal bone.
  • Promoting balance and harmony with other facial features.
  • A positive effect on the overall beauty of the face.
  • Correcting the health problems associated with the deviated septum.
  • Improving breathing.

Rhinoplasty experiences in Turkey

The recovery after rhinoplasty takes one to two weeks. Most patients usually return to normal life within 10 days.
