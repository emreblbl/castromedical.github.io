What is Novel Coronavirus?

Coronavirus Covid 19 is an infectious respiratory disease that can spread from person to person. The causing virus is a new crown-shaped virus that was first identified during an investigation into a recent outbreak in Wuhan, China.

Most people with Covid 19 virus suffer from mild to moderate respiratory disease and recover without the need for special treatment. It is more likely to affect the elderly, and those with underlying medical problems such as cardiovascular disease, diabetes, chronic respiratory disease, and cancer.

Novel Coronavirus, symptoms, and causes

According to the World Health Organization, the most common symptoms of Covid 19 are fever, fatigue, and a dry cough. Some patients may also experience a runny nose, sore throat, nasal congestion, aches, pain or diarrhea.

How does Coronavirus transmit?

The virus is believed to be from an animal origin, but it spread mainly from an infected person to a healthy person.

Does Coronavirus spread by cargo

Peoples can get Covid 19 by touching a surface or object containing the virus and then touching their mouth, nose, or perhaps their eyes, but this is not the main way to transmit the virus.

Can clothes spread Coronavirus?

Yes, clothes and towels can spread germs in general, and they can contribute in spreading the new virus as well.

There are 3 main ways to spread germs with clothes and towels:

  • When towels or bed covers are used by more than one person, germs can spread between them.
  • People who deal with dirty laundry, it is possible to spread germs on their hands.
  • When washing clothes, germs can spread between different elements (towels, clothes, etc.) during the washing process.

Does handshaking spread Coronavirus?

Covid 19 can be transmitted through handshaking from a person whose hand is contaminated by touching objects or surfaces containing the virus and then touching the nose or mouth, and for this, governments around the world recommend alternatives to shaking hands, kisses, or touching the nose with the nose to prevent the spread of the virus.

Is Cornavirus transmitted by air?

Reports are conflicting as to whether the new Coronavirus can travel through the air. Some studies indicate that this is possible and are only preliminary studies, and are contradicted by other research, as there were no viral particles of coronavirus in the room's air of Covid 19 patients in hospitals.

Can Coronavirus spread through food?

There is no evidence to suggest that Covid 19 is transmitted through food. The main cause of transmission is in direct contact with the infected. We, therefore, advise the food companies and consumers to maintain the highest standards of good hygiene and wash their hands regularly. It is worth noting that studies indicate that good cooking may kill the virus.

How to prevent Coronavirus

You can protect yourself and help prevent spreading the virus to others by:

Do

  • Wash your hands regularly for 20 seconds, with soap and water or alcohol-based hand rub
  • Cover your nose and mouth with a disposable tissue or flexed elbow when you cough or sneeze and dispose of immediately after use.
  • Avoid close contact (1 meter or 3 feet) with unwell people
  • Stay home and self-isolate from others in the household if you feel unwell
  • Follow state and health organizations instructions

Don't

  • Touch your eyes, nose, or mouth if your hands are not clean.
  • Smoke.
  • Go out of home until extremely necessary.
  • Attend gathering and crowded places.

Coronavirus prevention tips – World Health Organization (WHO)

To prevent infection and to slow transmission of COVID-19, world health organization recommend to do the following:

  • Wash your hands regularly with soap and water, or clean them with alcohol-based hand rub.
  • Maintain at least 1 meter (3feet) distance between you and people coughing or sneezing.
  • Avoid touching your face.
  • Cover your mouth and nose when coughing or sneezing.
  • Stay home if you feel unwell.
  • Refrain from smoking and other activities that weaken the lungs.
  • Practice physical distancing by avoiding unnecessary travel and staying away from large groups of people.

Is Coronavirus curable?

According to the World Health Organization, there is no medicine intended to prevent or treat coronavirus, but people need palliative care to help them breathe.

If you have mild symptoms, stay home until you recover. You can reduce symptoms by:

  • Rest and sleep
  • Stay warm
  • Drink plenty of fluids
  • Use a humidifier or hot shower to help relieve sore throats and coughs.

Is Coronavirus deadly?

Most infected people with Coronavirus COVID 19 recover at home, while some need hospitalization.

However, the disease called COVID-19 is fatal for several patients, and their percentage reaches 3.4%. Where studies indicate that people with chronic diseases such as heart disease, diabetes, cancer and the elderly are more likely to die because of this virus.

Stay at home: staying home is one of the best preventive measures of Corona

Now that the new Coronavirus has spread to societies around the world, phrases such as "social distancing" and "quarantine" appear in the media.

Social distancing is a unique way to prevent Cornavirus while it may be disappointing to hear many sporting events, events, festivals and other gatherings have been canceled, but there is a valid justification for these measures. These cancellations help stop or slow the spread of the disease, allowing the healthcare system to care for patients more easily over time.

An example of social distancing is the abolition of events that are likely to attract crowds. Intentional Social distancing separates people to avoid the spread of disease.
